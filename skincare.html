SKIN CARE


 
LA SHIELD

Description:

Lacto Calamine Oil Balance Lotion for Oily Skin contains Kaolin Clay, Zinc Oxide, and Glycerin as ingredients. 
It is a clay-based lotion.
Provides oil balance for up to 8-hrs through its unique 3-way action to give a soft and supple skin every day.

Role of active ingredients:
Kaolin Clay: Absorbs excess oil from the skin surface without removing the essential oil.
Zinc Oxide: Helps unclogs pores by preventing the build-up of pimple-causing germs.
Glycerin: Helps retain natural moisture in the skin and prevents excessive drying of skin.

Direction for use:
1.Shake the lotion bottle before use.
2.Take a little lotion in the palm and apply it in dots on the face and neck area.
3.Gently spread it all over, rub it on the skin for at least 20-30 seconds.
4.For best results, use Lacto Calamine lotion twice a day

Storage instructions:
Store in cool and dry place
Protect from direct sunlight
Safety information:
For external use only
Read the instruction leaflet/label carefully before use
Keep out of the reach and sight of children
